The Barbie Movie!

Greta Gerwig's "Barbie" took the world by storm in 2023, becoming a cultural phenomenon and box office smash. The film follows the iconic doll as she leaves Barbieland for the real world, grappling with existential questions and societal expectations. With Margot Robbie as the charismatic Barbie and Ryan Gosling as her charming Ken, the movie captivated audiences with its vibrant visuals, witty humor, and surprisingly thought-provoking themes.

Our seats are comfy!

With a gentle push of a button, the chair transforms into a personalized haven, adjusting to your perfect angle for optimal viewing. Our popcorn is tasty!

The aroma of freshly popped popcorn is synonymous with the cinema experience, drawing you in with its buttery allure. The warmth of the popcorn melts in your mouth, leaving behind a lingering buttery flavor that begs for another handful.
